WHY DOES THIS EXIST?

Let's be honest: mental health tools for developers are too clinical. They don't speak our language. They don't understand the pain of a production bug at 2 AM. They don't know what it's like to cry over CSS centering.

Developers cope with memes, dark humor, and Twitter doomscrolling. We needed a break experience that actually feels like it was made BY developers, FOR developers.

TouchGrass is a low-friction, culturally relatable micro-break that uses absurdity to help you laugh at your own burnout. Because sometimes, the best therapy is realizing you're destined to be a Catfish Farmer.

  • No signup. No tracking. No cookies (except the edible ones).
  • Takes 2 minutes — less time than your CI pipeline.
  • 100% browser-based. Works on your phone while you hide in the bathroom.
  • Ends with a genuine motivational quote. We're funny, but we care.
